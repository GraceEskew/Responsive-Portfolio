About Me

Grace "Gracie" Eskew was born in Houston, TX in September of 1986, Due to the age differnece between her and her siblings, Gracie grew to love hobbies that she could do on her own. From an early age, she fostered a love for art, drama, reading, exploring anything with more than 3 trees, and expressed an early interest in not only playing video games, but their production.

In the summer of 2000, Gracie moved to Plano, TX with her mother. There she attended Williams High School and later Plano East Senior High School, and was active in multiple clubs & organizations. Due to unfortnate circumstances, she found herself homeless at 17. Quickly, she moved 6 hours west Midland, TX, and reenrolled in high school; ultimately finished the 2004 - 2005 academic year in 3 months. Upon graduating, she attended Midland Community College, focusing on art and history. There, she became an active member in several clubs, often serving as represenation at club rushes, meetings, and homecoming.

Gracie currently resides back in Dallas, TX with her husband and 2 cats; Saki and Hina. She is a member of the Women Who Code DFW chapter, and spends her free time rucking, cooking, playing video games, and maintaining her Instagram account.
